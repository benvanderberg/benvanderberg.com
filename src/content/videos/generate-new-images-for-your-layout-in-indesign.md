This video shows how designers can generate images from within Adobe InDesign using Firefly's generative AI, keeping the entire layout workflow in one place. Instead of switching to a separate tool to source or create imagery, users can prompt Firefly and place results directly into their document. The tutorial demonstrates how this integration accelerates the design process for editorial and marketing layouts.
