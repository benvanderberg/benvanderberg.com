Adobe Firefly's Structure Reference feature lets you upload an image — in this case a logo — and use its shapes and layout to guide how Firefly composes the generated output. The video shows how to upload a logo as the structure reference, write a text prompt for the desired content and style, and then generate images that follow the logo's structural form while taking on a completely different visual treatment. A useful technique for creating branded variations, concept art, or stylized derivatives of existing mark.
