A Paper Clips episode introducing Document Builder, a new feature in Adobe Acrobat Sign for Salesforce that lets users build and generate agreement documents directly from Salesforce data — no external Word template required. The demo shows how to set up a Document Builder template, map Salesforce fields, generate the document, and route it for e-signature, all within the Salesforce environment.
