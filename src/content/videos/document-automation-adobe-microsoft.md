A look at how Adobe and Microsoft have partnered to bring document automation into the Microsoft ecosystem. The video covers the integrations available across Adobe Sign, Adobe PDF Services, and Microsoft products including Teams, SharePoint, Power Automate, Power Apps, and Dynamics — showing how organizations that are already invested in Microsoft 365 can add e-signature and document generation capabilities without leaving their existing tools.
