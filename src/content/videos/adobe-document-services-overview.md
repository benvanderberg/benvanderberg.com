An introduction to the full Adobe Document Services platform and the APIs that make up the suite. The video covers what each API does — Document Generation for merging data into templates, PDF Services for creating and manipulating PDFs, PDF Extract for pulling structured content out of PDFs, PDF Embed for displaying PDFs in web apps, and Adobe Sign for e-signatures — and how they fit together to automate document workflows end to end.
