A demonstration of the Generative Recolor feature in Adobe Illustrator, which uses Adobe Firefly to apply AI-suggested color schemes to vector artwork. The video shows how to select artwork, enter a prompt or mood description, and let Firefly generate harmonious color palettes. This feature dramatically speeds up the process of exploring color variations for logos, icons, and illustrations.
