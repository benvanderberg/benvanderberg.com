This tutorial covers how to leverage Adobe Firefly's generative AI capabilities to create custom transition assets for use in Adobe Premiere Pro. Instead of relying on stock transitions, editors can generate unique visual effects that match their project's style. The video walks through the workflow from generating assets in Firefly to applying and fine-tuning them as transitions in Premiere.
