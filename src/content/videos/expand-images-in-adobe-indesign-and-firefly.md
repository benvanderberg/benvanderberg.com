This video demonstrates how to use Firefly's generative image expansion capability directly inside Adobe InDesign, allowing designers to extend photos and illustrations to fill larger frames or different aspect ratios. The feature intelligently fills in the expanded areas to match the original image's content and style. This is especially useful for repurposing assets across different layout sizes without reshooting or manually retouching.
