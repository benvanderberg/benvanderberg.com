This video demonstrates how to use Composition and Style reference features in Adobe Firefly to produce high-quality hero images. By supplying a composition reference, users can guide the layout and structure of generated images, while style references control the visual aesthetic and tone. Together, these tools give marketers and designers precise control over Firefly's output for campaign and editorial use.
