A Paper Clips episode showing how to embed document generation and electronic signature directly inside a Microsoft Power App. Users can trigger the creation of a PDF from app data, then send it for signing through Adobe Sign — all within the Power Apps interface. The demo covers connecting the Adobe PDF Services and Adobe Sign connectors, setting up the flow, and the end-user experience of generating and signing a document in a single session.
