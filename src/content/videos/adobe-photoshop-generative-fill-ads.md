A walkthrough of using Adobe Photoshop's Generative Fill feature to customize images for advertising use cases. The video covers selecting areas of a photo, prompting Generative Fill to add or replace elements, and adapting a single image into multiple ad variations. This approach helps marketing teams produce on-brand ad assets faster without extensive photo retouching.
