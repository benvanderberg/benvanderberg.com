A demonstration of using Adobe Sign web forms to collect forbearance agreement signatures digitally — allowing financial institutions to provide customers with a fully electronic way to request loan payment relief. The video shows how to set up the web form, what the customer-facing signing experience looks like, and how completed agreements are tracked and stored. A timely use case for lenders offering pandemic-era relief options.
