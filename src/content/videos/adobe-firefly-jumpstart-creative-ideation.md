Adobe Firefly's generative AI tools make it easier than ever to jumpstart the early stages of a creative project. Instead of staring at a blank canvas, you can rapidly generate visual concepts, explore different directions, and arrive at strong ideas faster.

This demo walks through how Firefly can be used to produce a wide range of initial concepts from simple text prompts, giving creative teams a starting point to react to and refine. It's a practical look at how AI-assisted ideation fits into real creative workflows without replacing the human judgment at the center of them.
