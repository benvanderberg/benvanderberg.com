Adobe Firefly Creative Production lets you remove or swap backgrounds across a large set of images in just a few clicks. Upload JPG, PNG, or TIFF files from your local device or connect directly to cloud storage, then choose to either strip backgrounds to transparency or replace them all with a single new background image. The job runs in the cloud and you get an email when it's done — then download a zip of all the processed assets.
