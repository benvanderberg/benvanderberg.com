Adobe Firefly added Google Gemini Flash 2.5 as a selectable image generation model, letting you switch between Adobe's native Firefly models and Google's Gemini directly inside the Firefly interface. The video demonstrates how to choose Gemini Flash 2.5, what the output looks like compared to Firefly's own models, and how the "Nano Banana" variant fits into Adobe's growing multi-model AI strategy.
