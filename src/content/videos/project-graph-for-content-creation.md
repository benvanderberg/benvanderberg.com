This video introduces Project Graph, a tool built to help content creators map out and manage their creative workflows. It demonstrates how assets, tasks, and dependencies can be visualized as a connected graph, making it easier to track progress across complex projects. The demo shows how Project Graph can simplify coordination between different stages of content production.
