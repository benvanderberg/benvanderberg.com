A tutorial on creating Style Kits in Adobe Firefly, which act as reusable recipes for generating on-brand imagery. The video explains how to capture the visual style of existing assets, save them as a kit, and apply them to new Firefly generations. Style Kits help teams maintain brand consistency when producing AI-generated content at scale.
