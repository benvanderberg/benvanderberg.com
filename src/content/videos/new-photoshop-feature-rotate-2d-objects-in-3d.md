This video introduces a new Photoshop feature that allows users to rotate 2D objects in three-dimensional space directly within the canvas. The Rotate Object tool gives designers precise control over perspective and angle without leaving Photoshop. The Harmonize feature is also covered, automatically matching the lighting and color of a repositioned object to blend naturally with the rest of the composition.
