Adobe Firefly's Effects panel gives you a library of visual style presets that guide the look of your generated images without requiring detailed prompt engineering. The video walks through the Effect categories — including color and tone, lighting, camera angle, and artistic styles like mosaic, neon, or pointillism — and shows how combining them shapes the output. A practical way to move from a rough text prompt to a precise visual result faster.
