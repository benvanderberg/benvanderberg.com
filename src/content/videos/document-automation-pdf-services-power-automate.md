A Paper Clips episode introducing the Adobe PDF Services connector for Microsoft Power Automate. The video demonstrates how non-developers can build automated document workflows using Power Automate's visual flow builder — operations like converting HTML or Word files to PDF, merging documents, and applying OCR — without writing a single line of code. A good entry point for business users who want to automate PDF-heavy processes.
