Photoshop Beta's Style reference lets you supply an image that guides the visual output of Generative Fill — pulling its color palette, lighting direction, texture, and tonal balance into your generated pixels instead of relying purely on text prompts. The tutorial walks through the full workflow: uploading a style reference, making a selection, and running Generative Fill to produce results that look like they belong in the same composition.
